Frequently asked

How accurate is AI-generated Vedic astrology? ↓
Accuracy depends on the quality of birth data and the prompt. Jyotish AI uses the most advanced AI model available (Claude Sonnet), with a 25-year senior astrologer persona and explicit instructions to cite specific planets, nakshatras, and dasha periods in every claim. It is not a human astrologer, but for timing windows, planetary analysis, and pattern recognition, it outperforms most generic software.
What if I don't know my exact birth time? ↓
Mention "time unknown" in the time field. The AI will note this and rely on planetary positions that don't change with birth time (Moon sign may be uncertain, but Sun, Mars, Saturn, Rahu positions and Dasha periods are still calculable). The reading will be partial but still highly useful.
How long does the reading take? ↓
Typically 3–7 minutes for all 23 modules. You see results appearing in real time as each module completes. The more modules selected, the longer it takes.
Is my data stored anywhere? ↓
No. Your birth data and images are sent directly from your browser to the AI provider. Nothing is stored on Jyotish AI's servers. Your API key (if used) never leaves your browser session.
What payment methods are accepted? ↓
All major options via Razorpay — UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay), net banking, and wallets. Payment is processed in seconds and your reading starts immediately.
Can I get a refund? ↓
Because the reading is generated and delivered instantly, we do not offer refunds after the reading is generated. If you experience a technical failure (reading didn't load, PDF failed), contact us on WhatsApp and we'll regenerate it at no charge.
Can I use my own API key instead of paying? ↓
Yes. Enter your Claude, Gemini, Groq, or other API key in Step 4 and click "Use My Key (Free)". You pay the AI provider directly — typically ₹20–80 per full reading. All 23 modules, full PDF export, no restrictions.
