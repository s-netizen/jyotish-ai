QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

How is this different from other astrology reports?↓
Most reports analyse only your birth chart. Ours reads three additional layers: your handwriting (18 personality traits), both your palm lines (karmic and active destiny maps), and your face (physiognomic blueprint from Samudrika Shastra). The four systems are then cross-referenced into one coherent story. No other report does this.
How accurate is the reading?↓
Accuracy depends on the quality of your birth data and images. An exact birth time gives the most precise Lagna, Dasha periods, and divisional charts. Clear, well-lit images give the most detailed handwriting, palm, and face analysis. The more precise your inputs, the more specific the output. We recommend re-checking your birth time with family before submitting.
What technology generates the report?↓
The reading is generated using advanced computational analysis trained on classical Vedic texts, graphology systems, and Samudrika Shastra. Each report is computed fresh from your specific data — nothing is templated or pre-written. The analysis is then reviewed before delivery to ensure quality and consistency.
What if I don't know my exact birth time?↓
Select the closest approximate time and mention "approximate" in your question field. We will note this and work with planetary positions that do not depend on exact birth time. The report will still cover career, relationships, karma, and timing windows. The Lagna and some Dasha periods may be slightly less precise.
How long does delivery take?↓
Your report is delivered to your email within 4 to 5 hours of payment confirmation. We do not rush the process. Each report is checked before it goes out. If you have not received it after 6 hours, write to support@tatva.in and we will follow up immediately.
Is my data stored or shared?↓
Your birth data and images are used only to generate your reading. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose. Images are processed and not stored long-term. Your report data is retained only long enough to email you the PDF. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

All major options via Razorpay: UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay), net banking, and wallets. Payment is processed securely. We never see your card details.
