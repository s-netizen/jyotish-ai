Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Jyotish AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Information We Collect

When you use Jyotish AI, we may collect the following:

Account information: If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from those providers. If you sign in with mobile OTP, we collect your phone number.

Birth data and personal details: Name, date of birth, time of birth, place of birth, and any optional details you provide (partner name, relocation city, health state). This data is used solely to generate your reading.

Uploaded images: Handwriting samples, palm photos, and face photos are processed in-session to generate readings. We do not store these images on our servers.

API keys: If you use Explorer mode, your AI provider API key is used only within your browser session. It is never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the Jyotish AI reading service. Birth data is sent to your chosen AI provider (Anthropic, Google, Groq, etc.) to generate your reading. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Jyotish AI uses the following third-party services:

Supabase: For user authentication and session management. Subject to Supabase's privacy policy.

AI Providers: Your reading data is sent to your chosen provider (Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, Groq, etc.) to generate responses. Each provider has their own privacy policy and data handling practices.

Netlify: For website hosting. Subject to Netlify's privacy policy.

Data Retention

Reading content is not stored server-side. Your account information (email or phone number) is retained in Supabase for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at sanchit99.sr@gmail.com.

Google OAuth

Jyotish AI uses Google OAuth for sign-in. We request access only to your name and email address. We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, contacts, or any other Google services.

Children's Privacy

Jyotish AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.

Contact
