Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Tatva ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Information We Collect

Personal details: Name, phone number, date of birth, time of birth, place of birth, current city, career, and the question you submit. This is used only to generate your reading.

Images: Handwriting samples, palm photographs, and face photos you upload. These are processed to generate your report and are not stored permanently after delivery.

Payment information: Payments are processed entirely by Razorpay. We do not see, store, or have access to your card or bank details at any point.

Email address: Collected to deliver your report. Not used for marketing without your consent.

How We Generate Your Report

Reports are generated using advanced computational analysis based on classical Vedic astrology, graphology, palmistry, and Samudrika Shastra principles. Your submitted data and images are processed through this system to produce a personalised analysis. The report is reviewed before delivery to ensure quality.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to generate and deliver your report. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties except as required to process payment (Razorpay) or deliver your email (our email service provider).

Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as needed to generate and deliver your report. Images are not stored after your report is delivered. You may request deletion of your data by emailing support@tatva.in.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact us at support@tatva.in to exercise these rights.

Third-Party Services

Razorpay: Handles all payment processing. Subject to Razorpay's privacy policy.

Cloudflare: Provides website hosting and security. Subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for persons under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For privacy questions: support@tatva.in
